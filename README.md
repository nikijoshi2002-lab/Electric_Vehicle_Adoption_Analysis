## Conclusion
This project provides a comprehensive analysis of electric vehicle adoption trends using data analytics tools. The analysis reveals that EV adoption has increased significantly over recent years, especially after 2018, indicating a strong shift toward sustainable transportation. Battery Electric Vehicles (BEVs) dominate the market compared to Plug-in Hybrid Vehicles (PHEVs), showing a clear consumer preference for fully electric options.
Among manufacturers, Tesla emerges as the market leader in terms of total vehicle registrations, while other companies are gradually expanding their presence. Additionally, the analysis highlights that certain cities contribute more heavily to EV adoption, suggesting regional differences in infrastructure, awareness, and demand. The study also shows that newer vehicle models offer improved electric range, reflecting technological advancements in the EV industry.

Overall, this project demonstrates how data-driven insights can help understand market trends, identify growth opportunities, and support decision-making in the evolving electric vehicle ecosystem.
